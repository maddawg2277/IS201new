Education

What I've learned

I have been so fortunate and blessed to have the opportunity to learn and study under incredible teachers and professors. They have all spent numerous hours planning lessons for my benefit. I have been lucky enough to learn not only academic lessons, but life lessons as well. Life lessons like how to invest, important conversations to have with your spouse before marriage, how to be better at time managment, how to create a budget, along with many more.

One of my favorite parts of the classes at Brigham Young University has been the challenge it creates. It has increased my problem solving skills and my determination to learn new things that are challenging and difficult. When the time comes that you need help on an assignment there are so many resources that are eager to help. TA's are readily available along with Professors.

I have absolutely loved my religion classes at BYU. The knowledge that the Professors have and the insights that they share have impacted my own way of thinking and deepened my own testimony on numerous accounts. I am so grateful to have had the opportunity to study at a University that prioritizes religion courses and staffs them with highly qualified individuals.
